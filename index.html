sensors information

A security sensor is a device used to detect various types of physical intrusions, environmental changes, or unauthorized activities in a secured area. These sensors are commonly found in security systems for homes, businesses, and other protected environments. They help enhance safety by providing real-time monitoring and triggering alarms when suspicious activity is detected.
